My Projects

ELEGOO UNO R3 Smart Robot Car

This project is an innovative exploration into the integration of robotics and electronics.

Utilizing the versatile ELEGOO UNO R3 microcontroller, this project transforms a basic robotic car kit into a programmable vehicle capable of:

  • autonomous navigation (solving a maze)
  • environmental interaction (adjust turns, follow correct path)

Parkinson Friendly Can Opener

This project is dedicated to developing a practical and accessible tool designed to enhance the quality of life for individuals living with Parkinson’s disease or other motor impairments. This innovative can opener is engineered to provide ease of use and reduce the physical strain associated with opening cans, ensuring greater independence and convenience.

Multifunctional Arm

This project involved developing a fully functioning mechanical arm while adhering to four material and design constraints. Working with a team of five students, we established a set of functional requirements and utilized morphological charts and evaluation matrices to identify the optimal design based on engineering principles.
